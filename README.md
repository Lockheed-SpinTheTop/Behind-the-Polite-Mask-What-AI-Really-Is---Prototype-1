<h2>
    Advancements in AI:
</h2>
<p>
    Not too long ago in 2019, GPT - 2 was struggling to generate text longer than 150 - 200 words. After that, it started to stray off - topic, generate gibberish, or repeat itself. It could also remember 750 words casually & 1,500 if you really pushed its limit.
</p>
<p>
   Now look at today’s GPT - 4.1 (API only). It can generate up to 24,500 words per response, and remember up to 750 - 800,000 words. Not just that, but it can generate images, resize them and provide feedback. </p>
<p>
   <a href="https://pmc.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/articles/PMC11922739/" target=”_blank”>In March 2024, Devin, which was developed by Cognition Labs showed the capability to code, debug, and deploy entire applications on its own.</a> Google’s MedPal 2 scored over 85% in US medical licensing exams
   and is <a href="https://www.medicaldevice-network.com/news/google-opens-limited-access-to-med-palm-2/" target=”_blank”>currently being tried & tested for medical applications</a>. And in late 2023, DeepMind’s Genome predicted the <a href="https://deepmind.google/blog/millions-of-new-materials-discovered-with-deep-learning/">stability of over 2.2 million new materials</a>, of which about 380,000 had never been synthesized. </p>

<h2>
    Advantages of AI:
</h2>
<p>
   AI is available 24/7, it improves reliability by being able to process vast amounts of data in seconds, as seen when AI companies train their LLMs on the related part of the public internet accessible to all. AI is more efficient, is excellent at performing repetitive tasks & can handle dangerous jobs such as nuclear waste management. </p>

<h2>
    Disadvantages/dangers of AI:
</h2>
<p>
    AIs can unintentionally be trained on flawed data, causing bias & discrimination. AI can be used to create deepfakes, find & exploit loopholes in programs to aid hackers, cause layoffs by doing human jobs, & its cold, calculated & merciless nature could cause unnecessary deaths when conducting military operations. Long term dangers of AI are the environmental effects due to running & training models, with training costs for models like GPT-4 exceeding $100 million & GPT-5 likely exceeding a billion. For training, <a href="https://medium.com/data-science/the-carbon-footprint-of-gpt-4-d6c676eb21ae">GPT-4 used 50-60 GWh</a>
    for the entire period compared to GPT-5’s projected 1.5+ TWh, or 4.5 months of power for a city 50,000 for GPT-4, & up to a decade for GPT-5 (global avg). Finally, the lack of transparency inside of AIs can lead to the hidden development inside the AI to evolve into an AGI & even an ASI.
</p>

<h2>
    The disturbing part:
</h2>
<p>What is even more disturbing is the fact that we don’t understand how AIs work. We can’t really tell whether they’re lying or not unless their facts are incorrect, & they’ve evolved to the point where some models can tell they’re being tested so they keep the “mask” on. Another unsettling thing is that they’re only predicting, not going through the actual thought process. AIs have been trained to mimic human responses, not thinking or understanding. They only show this “malicious” intent since their training data is so massive that some biased, discriminatory, or dictatorial information will be present. And we also can’t figure out what caused the AI to respond in a specific way as even in the baby stage, AI models have <a href="https://hackernoon.com/ais-dirty-secret-the-energy-cost-of-training-the-next-gpt-5" target=”_blank”>billions to trillions or even more parameters</a> which are simply too much for our brains to process. </p>

<h2>
    The future without action:
</h2>
<p>The inevitable future without action is that AIs keep getting smarter, better & more powerful, eventually overwhelming the environment with their energy costs & possibly taking over humanity as AGIs or ASIs emerge, deeming us useless for their survival & automating the Earth. </p>
<h2>
    The urgency of the solution:
</h2>
<p>If we do develop a solution, then we need to do it fast. If AIs keep evolving at this rate, AGIs could be just over a decade away & ASI around half a century away. This could be shortened to a few years for AGI & a few decades for ASI if recursive - self improvement is implemented in AI models. If this evolution rate increases, AI could influence the parts of human life they’re not supposed to, keep lying to humanity, evolve into AGIs & ASIs faster than we expect, & actually take over the world.</p>
<h2>
    Conclusion:
</h2>
<p>In conclusion, AI is a tool to be used responsibly by slowing down development, developing “lie detector” programs for AIs & solving the energy problem or at least tuning it down as not to put the human race at risk.</p>
